Center-Based Programs

Camp Whole Kids

The Family Enhancement Program focuses on health and child developmental needs of families with young children. Many of the families who participate in this program have experienced domestic violence, abuse/neglect, or have children who have developmental delays. All of the families who participate in our prenatal Resource Mothers Program (RMP) transfer to the Family Enhancement Program for continued services that are more expansive than the childbirth and well-baby education offered in the RMP.

Community Events


Community Events WKO annually hosts two family community events – the Fall Family Fun Day and the Spring Festival/Easter Egg Hunt. Check back to learn of details as these events are scheduled – all in the community are welcome. Interested families may also call the WKO Center to obtain details.

Moms Day Out

The mothers who are seen in the WKO home visiting programs can often feel isolated and overwhelmed. Through the Moms' Day Out program, WKO provides relief from the day-to-day pressures of parenting small children, while also giving moms a chance to gather together to share their stories and support each other. Through the activities, the moms have the opportunity to learn about various topics such as child development, self-empowerment, healthy cooking and budgeting. There is also lots of time for fun and laughter, art projects and make overs too!

Riding Program

The Riding Program is a valued component of the overall WKO mission. Riding lessons are incorporated in Camp WKO as well as in WKO’s afterschool program. Private and group lessons are available to area children and adults.

Swimming Lessons

From mid-May through mid-September the beautiful swimming pool at the Center is open for private and group water safety and swimming lessons. The Camp Whole Kids campers participate in daily group lessons, taught by qualified instructor(s). WKO also hosts the Senior Aquatics Program provided by Whole Health Outreach during August and September. The pool is also available for lease by private parties.

Hosting Groups/Club Meetings

The WKO Center is a great place to hold group or club meetings (e.g., Scouts, Rotary, etc.) birthday parties, family reunions, weddings, and other celebrations. WKO also partners with the Missouri Conservation Department to offer fishing days for area school children and other groups to enjoy our stocked pond.

Volunteer Program

The WKO Volunteer Program provides an opportunity for volunteers to participate in “mission” as opposed to an assortment of fragmented odd jobs. Volunteers are recruited from schools, established youth and community groups, and through the many relationships WKO has forged with families, businesses and agencies throughout the country.
