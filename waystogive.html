WAYS TO GIVE

TYPES OF GIFTS

METHOD OF GIVING

BENEFITS TO DONOR

Outright gift

(includes tribute gifts, and gifts for tax credits)

Cash, appreciated securities (stocks and bonds)

Donor transfers assets to Whole Kids Outreach online or by mail.

Tax deductible to the full extent of the law

Gifts of stocks and bonds help a donor avoid capital gains tax

Donors qualifying for tax credit programs are eligible for additional state tax credits

Employer Matching Gift

Cash, appreciated securities (stocks and bonds)

Send in a matching gift form from you or your spouse’s employer and WKO will complete and submit it for the contribution. Many companies also match retiree and spouse gifts.

Tax deduction only for individual’s gift portion

Corporation recognized as donor

Pledge

Cash, appreciated securities (stocks and bonds)

Written agreement with WKO with a promise to make several contributions over a short-term period.

Tax deduction as each contribution on the pledge is made

Planned Giving

Cash, appreciated securities (stocks and bonds)

A provision in an estate plan such as a bequest in a will to transfer assets to Whole Kids Outreach

Control over distribution of estate

Avoidance of inheritance tax

Gifts-in-Kind

A vast array of items is needed for our many programs. See our most up-to-date wish list and more explanations.

Bring or ship items to WKO or request assistance for transporting.

Donated items are also needed for the Share A Toy Christmas Store and WKO’s annual auction.

Tax-deductible to the full extent of the law.

Value of gift is determined by donor and financial adviser

Volunteer Time

Service projectson-site or off-site for teens and adults.

Donate your time to help a particular program at WKO.

Satisfaction of directly helping the children and families served

Some volunteer hours may have tax benefits – consult your tax adviser

Work for WKO helps teens and others satisfy community service requirements for school and church

Special Events

Cash or appreciated securities (stocks and bonds)

Attend a special event to raise support for WKO and/or be a sponsor of an event. WKO’s major event is an annual dinner auction.

Tax-deductible to the full extent of the law

Sponsors are recognized at the event

Have a great time in the company of others who also support the mission of WKO

Third Party Events

Cash or appreciated securities (stocks and bonds) or gifts-in-kind, dependent on event

Host an event to benefit WKO or have a party and ask for gifts that will be donated to help WKO (such as for the Share a Toy Christmas Store or for baby items).

Special recognition as a supporter of WKO
